Q: What are your working hours?
A: Our standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday, with a one-hour lunch break. Flexible timing options may be available depending on your role and department.

Q: How many vacation days do employees get?
A: Full-time employees receive 15 paid vacation days per year, which increases to 20 days after 3 years of service. Part-time employees receive prorated vacation based on their scheduled hours.

Q: What sick leave benefits are available?
A: Employees accrue 1 day of sick leave per month, totaling 12 days per year. Unused sick leave can be carried over to the next year, up to a maximum of 30 days.

Q: Do you offer health insurance?
A: Yes, we offer comprehensive health insurance including medical, dental, and vision coverage. Benefits begin on the first day of the month following your start date.

Q: Is there a probation period?
A: Yes, new employees have a 3-month probation period. During this time, performance reviews are conducted at 30, 60, and 90 days.

Q: How do I request time off?
A: Time off requests should be submitted through our HR portal at least two weeks in advance. Emergency time off can be arranged by contacting your manager directly.

Q: What holidays does the company observe?
A: We observe 10 paid holidays per year including New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving (2 days), Christmas (2 days), and 2 floating holidays of your choice.

Q: How do I report an absence?
A: Report any absence by calling the HR hotline at extension 1234 or emailing absence@company.com before your scheduled start time.

Q: What is the dress code?
A: We maintain a business casual dress code. However, certain departments may have specific requirements for safety or client meetings.

Q: Can I work remotely?
A: Yes, we offer flexible remote work options. Employees can work remotely up to 2 days per week with manager approval, subject to business needs.
