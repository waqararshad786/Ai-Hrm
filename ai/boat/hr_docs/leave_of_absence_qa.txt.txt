# APPLY FOR LEAVE

Q: How do I apply for leave?
A: Dashboard > "Apply Leave" > Pick dates > Select leave type > Add reason > Submit

Q: where i apply for leave ?
A: you apply leave in leave page 


Q: Where to apply leave?
A: Attendance Dashboard > "Apply Leave"

Q: Leave application link?
A: Go to Attendance Dashboard and click "Apply Leave"

Q: How to request time off?
A: Dashboard > Apply Leave > Fill details > Submit

# LEAVE LIMITS

Q: How many leaves per month?
A: Maximum 2 leaves per month normally

Q: Can I take more than 2 leaves?
A: Yes, but need manager's special approval

Q: Emergency leave limit?
A: Contact HR directly for emergencies

# NOTICE PERIOD

Q: How many days before should I apply?
A: • Planned leave: 7 days before
  • Emergency: Tell manager same day
  • Medical leave: 30 days if possible

Q: Last minute leave application?
A: Call manager immediately, then apply in dashboard

# SICK LEAVE

Q: How to take sick leave?
A: Dashboard > Select "Sick Leave" > Add reason > Submit

Q: Doctor note required?
A: Yes if sick leave is more than 2 days

Q: Sick leave without certificate?
A: Only for 1-2 days

# CASUAL LEAVE

Q: How to take casual leave?
A: Dashboard > Select "Casual Leave" > Apply 2 days before > Get approval

Q: Planned time off?
A: Use "Casual Leave" option, apply 7 days before

# EMERGENCY LEAVE

Q: Sudden emergency leave?
A: 1. Call manager now
  2. Apply in dashboard with "Emergency" tag
  3. Submit proof within 24 hours

Q: Family emergency leave?
A: Call manager, then apply as emergency leave

# MEDICAL LEAVE

Q: Long term medical leave?
A: Contact HR, fill medical forms, provide doctor's note

Q: What is FMLA?
A: Medical leave for serious health conditions

Q: How to apply FMLA?
A: Tell HR 30 days before, complete medical forms

Q: Disability leave?
A: 60% salary for up to 12 weeks with medical proof

# OTHER LEAVES

Q: Leave for family death?
A: 3-5 days paid bereavement leave, tell HR relationship

Q: Sabbatical leave?
A: 4 weeks paid after 5 years service, apply 6 months before

Q: Court duty leave?
A: Paid leave, show court paper to HR

Q: Military leave?
A: Unpaid up to 5 years, benefits continue, show orders

Q: Unpaid leave?
A: Ask manager and HR 30 days before, subject to work needs

# DURING LEAVE

Q: Benefits during leave?
A: Continue, you pay your part before or after leave

Q: Cancel approved leave?
A: Dashboard > "My Leaves" > Select > "Cancel" > Tell manager

Q: Extend leave?
A: Apply extension in dashboard, call manager, wait for approval

# RETURN FROM LEAVE

Q: How to return from leave?
A: • Sick leave: Submit medical clearance
  • Other leave: Tell manager you're back
  • Check dashboard for correct status

Q: First day back?
A: Inform manager, check attendance in dashboard
