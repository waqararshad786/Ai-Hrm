# PAYDAY

Q: When is payday?
A: Every other Friday

Q: What are pay dates?
A: Bi-weekly on Fridays

Q: When will I get paid?
A: Every other Friday

Q: What if payday is a holiday?
A: You get paid on the previous working day

# PAY SLIPS

Q: Where do I get my pay slip?
A: Employee Portal > "Payroll" > "View Pay Stubs"

Q: How to download pay slip?
A: Portal > Payroll > View Pay Stubs > Download PDF

Q: I need my salary slip. Where to find?
A: Login to Employee Portal, go to Payroll section

Q: Can I get paper pay slip?
A: Yes, request from HR for paper copy

Q: How to check my salary online?
A: Portal > Payroll > View Pay Stubs

Q: View my last paycheck?
A: Portal > Payroll > Payment History

# DIRECT DEPOSIT

Q: How to set up direct deposit?
A: Portal > Payroll Settings > Direct Deposit > Add bank details

Q: Where do I put my bank details for salary?
A: Portal > Payroll Settings > Direct Deposit

Q: Can I split salary in two accounts?
A: Yes, you can split % between checking and savings

Q: How many bank accounts for salary?
A: You can add up to 3 different accounts

# TAX & FORMS

A: Portal > Tax Documents > Download W-2

Q: Old W-2 required. How to get?

# TIME & OVERTIME

Q: How to track my work hours?
A: Clock in/out daily in time tracking system

Q: How to request overtime?
A: Submit in time system, get manager approval

Q: Overtime payment rate?
A: 1.5 times normal pay

Q: Need overtime approval. How?
A: Submit request 24 hours in advance in time system

# DEDUCTIONS

Q: What is deducted from my salary?
A: Tax, Social Security, Medicare, plus benefits you chose

Q: Why is tax deducted?
A: Federal and state tax as per law

Q: Change my deductions?
A: Update during open enrollment or within 30 days of life change
