Q: How is overtime calculated?
A: Overtime is paid at 1.5 times your regular hourly rate for hours worked beyond 40 hours per week. Overtime must be pre-approved by your manager.

Q: What retirement plans do you offer?
A: We offer a 401(k) plan with company matching up to 4% of your salary. Employees are eligible to enroll after 90 days of employment.

Q: Do you provide parental leave?
A: Yes, we offer 12 weeks of paid parental leave for birth or adoption. This can be taken anytime within the first year of the event.

Q: What education assistance programs are available?
A: We provide up to $5,000 per year in tuition reimbursement for job-related courses, and $10,000 for degree programs. Requires grade of C or better and one year of service.

Q: How does the bonus structure work?
A: Annual performance bonuses are based on individual and company performance, typically ranging from 5-15% of your base salary. Paid out in March each year.

Q: Are there wellness benefits?
A: Yes, we offer a wellness stipend of $500 per year for gym memberships, fitness classes, or wellness apps. We also provide an Employee Assistance Program (EAP).

Q: What is the salary review process?
A: Salary reviews occur annually in December, with adjustments effective in January. Performance reviews throughout the year contribute to these decisions.

Q: Do you offer commuter benefits?
A: Yes, we offer pre-tax commuter benefits for parking and transit passes. You can contribute up to the IRS monthly limit.

Q: What life insurance coverage is provided?
A: We provide basic life insurance coverage of 2x your annual salary at no cost. Additional coverage can be purchased.

Q: Are there referral bonuses?
A: Yes, employees receive $2,000 for referring candidates who are hired and complete 90 days of employment.
