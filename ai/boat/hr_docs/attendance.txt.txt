# MARKING ATTENDANCE

Q: Where do I mark my attendance?
A: Go to Attendance Dashboard: https://portal.company.com/attendance

Q: Where is the attendance dashboard?
A: https://portal.company.com/attendance

Q: How do I check in?
A: Login to dashboard and click "Check In" button

Q: How do I check out?
A: Login to dashboard and click "Check Out" button

Q: What are my work hours?
A: 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (Monday-Friday)

Q: What is the grace period?
A: You can check in until 9:15 AM without being late

# LATE ARRIVAL

Q: When am I considered late?
A: After 9:15 AM

Q: What happens if I'm late?
A: • 9:15-9:30 AM = Marked late
  • After 9:30 AM = 1 hour leave deducted
  • After 10:00 AM = Half day marked

Q: How do I report being late?
A: Dashboard > "Report Late Arrival" > Select reason > Submit

Q: I'm coming late today. What to do?
A: 1. Mark "Report Late Arrival" in dashboard
  2. Tell your manager

# ATTENDANCE PROBLEMS

Q: I forgot to mark attendance. What now?
A: Dashboard > "Missed Punch" within 24 hours > Add time and reason > Submit

Q: I forgot to check in/out. Help?
A: Go to "Missed Punch" option in dashboard and fix it

Q: Attendance system is down. What to do?
A: Email attendance@company.com with:
  • Your name and ID
  • Time you came/left
  • Error screenshot

Q: My attendance is wrong. How to fix?
A: Dashboard > "Request Correction" > Select date > Fix time > Submit

Q: Who do I contact for attendance help?
A: • System issues: IT Helpdesk (ext. 7777)
  • Wrong entries: attendance@company.com
  • Rules & policies: HR (ext. 1234)

# WORK FROM HOME

Q: How to mark WFH attendance?
A: Dashboard > Select "Work From Home" > Check in at 9 AM > Check out at 5 PM

Q: I'm working from home. How to mark?
A: Same as office - use dashboard and select "WFH" mode

# CHECK ATTENDANCE

Q: How to check my attendance record?
A: Dashboard > "Summary Report"

Q: How many days was I present?
A: Check Dashboard > "Summary Report"

Q: How many times was I late?
A: Dashboard > "Summary Report" shows all late entries

Q: Check my leave balance?
A: Dashboard > "Summary Report" > Leave Balance section

Q: Show my attendance summary?
A: Go to Dashboard > "Summary Report" for complete details
