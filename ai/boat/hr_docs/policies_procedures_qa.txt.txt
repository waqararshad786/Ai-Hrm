Q: What is the company policy on harassment?
A: We have a zero-tolerance policy for harassment of any kind. Report incidents to HR, your manager, or through our anonymous ethics hotline at 1-800-555-0123.

Q: How do I report a workplace injury?
A: Report any workplace injury immediately to your supervisor and complete an incident report within 24 hours. Seek medical attention if needed.

Q: What is the internet usage policy?
A: Company internet should be used primarily for work purposes. Limited personal use is acceptable but should not interfere with productivity or access inappropriate content.

Q: How do I request a reasonable accommodation?
A: Submit accommodation requests to HR with supporting documentation. We'll engage in an interactive process to identify appropriate accommodations.

Q: What is the policy on conflicts of interest?
A: Employees must disclose any potential conflicts of interest, including outside employment, business relationships, or personal connections with vendors.

Q: How do I report ethics violations?
A: Report ethics concerns through our confidential hotline, ethics@company.com, or directly to HR. Retaliation against reporters is strictly prohibited.

Q: What is the data protection policy?
A: All employees must complete data privacy training annually. Handle customer and company data according to our Information Security Policy and relevant regulations.

Q: How do I request equipment for disability?
A: Submit a workplace accommodation request through HR. We'll work with you and IT to provide necessary assistive technologies or equipment.

Q: What happens in a reduction in force?
A: RIF decisions are based on business needs and performance. Affected employees receive severance packages and outplacement support.

Q: How do I report a data breach?
A: Immediately report suspected data breaches to IT Security at security@company.com and disconnect affected systems if possible.
