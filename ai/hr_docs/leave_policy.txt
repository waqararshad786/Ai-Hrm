LEAVE APPLICATION PROCESS - LSIT

HOW TO APPLY FOR LEAVE:
1. Log into LSIT HR portal
2. Click on "Leave Requests"
3. Fill out the leave application form with:
   - Leave type
   - Start date
   - End date
   - Reason for leave
4. Submit for approval

LEAVE SUBMISSION TIMELINE:
- Planned Leave: Submit at least 3 days in advance
- Sick Leave: Notify manager by 9:00 AM on the day of absence
- Emergency Leave: Notify manager immediately and submit application as soon as possible

LEAVE TYPES AND ALLOCATION:
| Leave Type | Allocation | Notes |
|------------|------------|-------|
| Annual Leave | 20 days per year | Planned vacation/personal time |
| Sick Leave | 10 days per year | Medical illness or appointment |
| Casual Leave | 5 days per year | Urgent personal work |
| Unpaid Leave | As per manager approval | When leave balance is exhausted |

FAQ:
Q: How do I apply for leave?
A: Log into LSIT HR portal, click on "Leave Requests", fill out the form with dates and reason, then submit.

Q: How many annual leave days do I get?
A: 20 days of annual leave per year.

Q: How many sick leave days are available?
A: 10 days of sick leave per year.

Q: What is casual leave?
A: Casual leave is 5 days per year for urgent personal work that cannot be planned in advance.

Q: How far in advance should I apply for planned leave?
A: Submit leave application at least 3 days in advance for planned leave.

Q: What if I am sick and cannot come to institute?
A: Notify your manager by 9:00 AM on the day of absence and apply for sick leave through HR portal.

Q: Can I apply for unpaid leave?
A: Yes, unpaid leave can be requested with manager approval when leave balance is exhausted.

Q: How do I check my leave balance?
A: Log into LSIT HR portal and go to Leave Balance section.

Q: Who approves leave requests?
A: Your manager/department head approves leave requests.

Q: What if I need to cancel or modify my leave?
A: Contact your manager and submit a new request or modification through HR portal.

Q: Can leave be carried forward to next year?
A: Check with HR or your manager regarding carry-forward policy.

Q: What if I have an emergency and cannot apply in advance?
A: Notify your manager immediately by phone or email, then submit leave application as soon as possible.

Q: Do I need to apply for leave if I am attending a training?
A: Training requests are separate. Discuss with manager about training vs leave application.

Q: What happens if I exceed my sick leave?
A: Additional sick days may be considered as unpaid leave with manager approval.

Q: Can I take half-day leave?
A: Yes, specify half-day leave in the application with timings.

Q: Where can I view my leave history?
A: Log into LSIT HR portal and go to Leave History section.

Q: Who do I contact for leave-related questions?
A: Email administration@lsituoe.edu.pk for any leave-related inquiries.

Q: What if my manager is unavailable to approve leave?
A: Contact the department head or email administration@lsituoe.edu.pk for assistance.

Q: Can I apply for leave through mobile app?
A: Yes, leave requests can be submitted through LSIT Connect mobile app as well.

Q: What documents are required for sick leave?
A: For sick leave exceeding 2 days, a medical certificate may be required. Check with manager.
