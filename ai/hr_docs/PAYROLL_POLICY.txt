PAYROLL POLICY - LSIT 💰

SALARY STRUCTURE:

Pay Cycle:
- Salary is processed between 5th and 7th of each month
- If dates fall on weekend/holiday, salary is processed on next working day
- Salary is credited directly to employee bank account

Salary Components:
| Component | Percentage |
|-----------|-----------|
| Basic Salary | 50% of total salary |
| House Rent Allowance | 30% of basic salary |
| Transport Allowance | 10% of basic salary |
| Utilities Allowance | 5% of basic salary |
| Medical Allowance | 5% of basic salary |

DEDUCTIONS:
| Deduction Type | Details |
|----------------|---------|
| Income Tax | As per government policy |
| Provident Fund | 10% of basic salary |
| Loan Installments | As per agreement |
| Unpaid Leave | Deducted for leave without pay |
| Late Check-in | Rs. 500 per late check-in (after 3 times in a month) |

PROVIDENT FUND:
- Employee: 10% of basic salary
- Employer: 10% of basic salary
- PF statement available quarterly

ANNUAL INCREMENT:
- Performance-based increment in July
- Range: 5% to 15% based on performance
- Confirmation increment: 5% after 6-month probation

KEY INFORMATION:
| Field | Details |
|-------|--------|
| Salary Date | 5th to 7th of each month |
| Payment Method | Direct bank transfer |
| Probation Period | 6 months |
| PF Employee | 10% of basic salary |
| PF Employer | 10% of basic salary |
| Payroll Queries | accounts@lsituoe.edu.pk |
| General Support | administration@lsituoe.edu.pk |

FAQ:
Q: When is salary paid?
A: Salary is credited between 5th and 7th of each month to your bank account.

Q: Where i check salary information?
A:  you can check salary in payroll 


Q: What if salary date is a holiday?
A: Salary is processed on the next working day.

Q: How is salary calculated?
A: Basic Salary (50%) + House Rent (30%) + Transport (10%) + Utilities (5%) + Medical (5%).

Q: What deductions are taken from salary?
A: Income Tax, Provident Fund (10% of basic), loan installments, unpaid leave, and late check-in deductions.

Q: What is late check-in deduction?
A: After 3 late check-ins in a month, Rs. 500 is deducted for each additional late check-in.

Q: What is Provident Fund?
A: PF is a savings scheme. You pay 10% of basic salary, LSIT matches with 10%. Money is saved for retirement.

Q: How can I check PF balance?
A: PF statements are available quarterly. Contact accounts department.

Q: When is annual increment given?
A: Increments are given in July based on performance.

Q: How much increment can I get?
A: Increment is 5% to 15% based on your performance rating.

Q: How do I get salary slip?
A: Salary slip is sent by email monthly. You can also download from LSIT HR portal.

Q: What if salary is not received?
A: Contact accounts@lsituoe.edu.pk immediately.

Q: How is unpaid leave deducted?
A: (Monthly Basic Salary ÷ Working Days) × Number of unpaid leave days.

Q: How do I update bank account?
A: Submit new bank details to accounts department with proof.

Q: Who to contact for payroll questions?
A: Email accounts@lsituoe.edu.pk for all payroll queries.

Q: What is probation period?
A: Probation is 6 months. After completion, you get 5% confirmation increment.

Q: Can I get salary advance?
A: Yes, in emergency cases with manager approval. Contact accounts department.

Q: How do I update tax information?
A: Submit tax declaration form to accounts department by March 31st.

Q: Where can I view salary history?
A: Log into LSIT HR portal and go to Payroll History section.

Q: Where I check salary history?
A: Log into LSIT HR portal and go to Payroll History section.
