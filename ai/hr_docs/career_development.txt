FAQ:
Q: What training programs are available at LSIT?
A: LSIT offers Technical Certifications (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud), Leadership Development Program, Soft Skills Workshops, and Mentorship Program.

Q: How do I apply for a training program?
A: First discuss with your manager/HOD, then submit request through LSIT HR portal, and get approval from department head.

Q: Can I apply for technical certifications?
A: Yes, LSIT offers AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud certification programs.

Q: What is the mentorship program?
A: Mentorship program provides one-on-one guidance from experienced faculty or staff members to support your professional growth.

Q: What career growth paths are available?
A: LSIT offers three career tracks: Individual Contributor (technical expertise), Management (leadership roles), and Technical Expert (subject matter expert).

Q: Who do I talk to about career development?
A: Discuss with your manager or department head first. For further queries, email administration@lsituoe.edu.pk.

Q: Are soft skills workshops offered?
A: Yes, soft skills workshops cover Communication, Teamwork, and Presentation Skills.

Q: How long does the approval process take?
A: Approval timeline varies. Check with your department head after submitting the request through HR portal.

Q: Can I enroll in multiple training programs?
A: Yes, you can apply for multiple programs based on your career goals and manager's approval.

Q: Is the leadership program for everyone?
A: Leadership Development Program is available for those interested in management and leadership roles.

Q: Where do I submit training requests?
A: Submit all training requests through the LSIT HR portal after discussing with your manager.

Q: Who approves training requests?
A: Department head provides final approval after manager recommendation.

Q: What if I need guidance on which career track to choose?
A: Discuss with your manager or participate in the mentorship program for career guidance.

Q: How can I contact HR for training-related questions?
A: Email administration@lsituoe.edu.pk for all training and career development inquiries.
