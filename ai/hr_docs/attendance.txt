ATTENDANCE POLICY - LSIT

WHERE TO MARK ATTENDANCE:
- Web Portal: https://lsit.edu.pk/attendance
- Mobile App: LSIT Connect

CHECK-IN AND CHECK-OUT PROCESS:
Check In:
- Staff must click "Check In" at the start of day
- Check In deadline: Before 10:00 AM daily
- Late Check In: Marked as delayed

Check Out:
- Staff must click "Check Out" at the end of day
- Required for accurate attendance tracking

FORGOTTEN ATTENDANCE:
- If staff forgets to mark Check In or Check Out
- Contact administration within 24 hours
- Email: administration@lsituoe.edu.pk
- Administration will rectify the attendance record
