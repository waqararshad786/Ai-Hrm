TITLE: Essential Tech Books for Pakistani Developers
KEYWORDS: books, reading, learning, technical books, career development, pakistan
SKILLS_REQUIRED: Self-learning, Reading Comprehension

Curated list of must-read books with Pakistani prices and availability:

Programming Fundamentals:
1. "Clean Code" by Robert Martin
   - Price: Rs. 2,200 - Rs. 3,500
   - Available: Liberty Books, Readings, Amazon.ae
   - Focus: Writing maintainable code
   - Level: Beginner to Intermediate

2. "The Pragmatic Programmer" by David Thomas
   - Price: Rs. 2,800 - Rs. 4,200
   - Available: Liberty Books, Paramount Books
   - Focus: Software craftsmanship
   - Level: All levels

System Design & Architecture:
3. "Designing Data-Intensive Applications" by Martin Kleppmann
   - Price: Rs. 4,500 - Rs. 6,500
   - Available: Order from Amazon.ae, Readings
   - Focus: Distributed systems
   - Level: Intermediate to Advanced

4. "System Design Interview" by Alex Xu (Vol 1 & 2)
   - Price: Rs. 3,500 - Rs. 5,000 (each)
   - Available: Amazon.ae, Digital formats
   - Focus: Interview preparation
   - Level: Intermediate

Career & Leadership:
5. "The Manager's Path" by Camille Fournier
   - Price: Rs. 2,200 - Rs. 3,500
   - Available: Liberty Books, Readings
   - Focus: Engineering management
   - Level: Senior+ engineers

6. "Soft Skills" by John Sonmez
   - Price: Rs. 1,800 - Rs. 2,800
   - Available: Paramount Books, Amazon.ae
   - Focus: Developer career guide
   - Level: All levels

Algorithms & Data Structures:
7. "Introduction to Algorithms" (CLRS)
   - Price: Rs. 6,000 - Rs. 9,000
   - Available: Special order, Amazon.ae
   - Focus: Comprehensive algorithms
   - Level: Advanced

8. "Cracking the Coding Interview" by Gayle McDowell
   - Price: Rs. 2,800 - Rs. 4,200
   - Available: Liberty Books, Readings
   - Focus: Interview preparation
   - Level: Intermediate
