TITLE: Tech Salary Trends 2024 - Pakistan
KEYWORDS: salary, compensation, trends, pakistan, tech salaries, pkr, monthly salary
MARKET_YEAR: 2024

Comprehensive monthly salary ranges for tech roles in Pakistan (in PKR):

Entry Level (0-2 years):
- Junior Software Engineer: Rs. 60,000 - Rs. 120,000
- Data Analyst: Rs. 50,000 - Rs. 100,000
- QA Engineer: Rs. 45,000 - Rs. 90,000
- DevOps Intern: Rs. 40,000 - Rs. 80,000
- Fresh Graduate (Top companies): Rs. 80,000 - Rs. 150,000

Mid Level (3-6 years):
- Software Engineer: Rs. 150,000 - Rs. 250,000
- Senior Software Engineer: Rs. 250,000 - Rs. 400,000
- Data Scientist: Rs. 200,000 - Rs. 350,000
- DevOps Engineer: Rs. 180,000 - Rs. 320,000
- Product Manager: Rs. 250,000 - Rs. 450,000
- Tech Lead: Rs. 400,000 - Rs. 650,000

Senior Level (7-12 years):
- Engineering Manager: Rs. 600,000 - Rs. 950,000
- Principal Engineer: Rs. 650,000 - Rs. 1,100,000
- Architect: Rs. 550,000 - Rs. 900,000
- Director of Engineering: Rs. 1,000,000 - Rs. 1,800,000

Leadership (12+ years):
- VP of Engineering: Rs. 1,800,000 - Rs. 3,000,000
- CTO: Rs. 2,500,000 - Rs. 5,000,000+

Company-wise comparisons:
- Multinationals (Afiniti, Systems Limited): 20-30% above market
- Local Product Companies (Arbisoft, Confiz): Market rate
- International Remote: $2,000 - $8,000/month (Rs. 550,000 - 2,200,000)
- Service Companies (Techlogix, Contour): 10-15% below product companies
- Startups: Variable, often lower base but equity

Location-wise variations:
- Karachi: Highest, 10-15% above national average
- Lahore: 5-10% above average
- Islamabad/Rawalpindi: At national average
- Other cities: 10-20% below average

Remote Work Impact:
- Full remote Pakistani companies: Same as location-based
- Hybrid roles: Slightly higher (5-10%) for flexibility
- International remote: 3-5x local salaries

Benefits beyond salary:
- Health insurance (family coverage worth Rs. 500,000 - 1,000,000)
- Bonus (1-3 months salary)
- Provident fund (8-12% of basic)
- Stock options (rare in Pakistan, common in startups)
- Education budget (Rs. 100,000 - 400,000/year)
- Internet/utility allowance (Rs. 10,000 - 30,000/month)
- Gym membership (Rs. 30,000 - 100,000/year)
