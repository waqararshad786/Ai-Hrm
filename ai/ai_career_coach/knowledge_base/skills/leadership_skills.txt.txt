TITLE: Engineering Leadership Skills
KEYWORDS: leadership, management, mentoring, communication, team lead, soft skills
SKILLS_REQUIRED: Communication, Mentoring, Conflict Resolution, Strategic Thinking, Decision Making

Essential leadership skills for Tech Leads and Engineering Managers:

1. Technical Leadership
   - Architecture decision making
   - Code review best practices
   - Technical debt management
   - Innovation encouragement

2. People Management
   - 1:1 meetings effectively
   - Performance reviews
   - Career growth planning
   - Mentoring and coaching
   - Feedback delivery (SBI model)

3. Project Management
   - Sprint planning and estimation
   - Risk management
   - Stakeholder communication
   - Priority setting
   - Resource allocation

4. Communication Skills
   - Technical presentations
   - Cross-team collaboration
   - Conflict resolution
   - Negotiation skills
   - Active listening

5. Strategic Thinking
   - Technical roadmap planning
   - Team structure optimization
   - Process improvement
   - Business alignment
